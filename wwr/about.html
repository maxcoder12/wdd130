About us

At Rapids, we are passionate about creating unforgettable white-water adventures! With over 29 Years of experience navigating some of the most scenic and exciting rivers, our team of expert guides is dedicated to providing both thrill-seekers and nature lovers with a safe and exhilarating experience. Whether you're new to rafting or a seasoned paddler, we offer customized trips that cater to all levels of expertise. Our mission is to connect you with the beauty of the outdoors while delivering the ultimate river adventure. Come join us and make memories that will last a lifetime!

Founded in 1995, Rapids started with a simple mission: to share the thrill and beauty of river rafting with adventure seekers from all walks of life. Our journey began on the rugged banks of the Colorado River, where a small group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts recognized the potential for offering guided trips through its stunning rapids and serene wilderness. What started as a weekend activity for friends soon grew into a full-fledged business, as word spread about the unique combination of excitement and natural beauty that our rafting trips provided.
